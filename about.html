About Reusable Rangoli

Reusable Rangoli Designs is a creative idea that makes traditional rangoli easier and cleaner to create. Instead of drawing rangoli from scratch every time, reusable designs allow you to create beautiful patterns quickly using stencils or templates.

This method saves time, reduces mess, and allows anyone to create stunning rangoli designs during festivals like Diwali, Holi, and other celebrations.

This website was designed and developed by Tanvi Patil as an interactive concept project for reusable rangoli stencil designs. The platform allows users to explore rangoli styles, view festival themed patterns, and experiment with designs using the Try Rangoli Designer tool.

Our goal is to make rangoli accessible for everyone while keeping the beauty of this traditional art alive.
