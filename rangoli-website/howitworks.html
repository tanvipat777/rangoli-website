How Reusable Rangoli Works

Creating beautiful rangoli has never been easier. Follow these simple steps:

1. Choose a Design

Select your favorite reusable rangoli stencil.

2. Place the Stencil

Place the stencil on the floor where you want the rangoli.

3. Add Colors

Fill the design with colorful rangoli powder.

4. Remove the Stencil

Lift the stencil carefully to reveal a perfect rangoli.
